We're Celebrating Recycle City's 25th Anniversary!

There's lots to do here - people and places to visit and plenty of ways to explore how all the city kids and adults reduce waste, reuse and repair things, recycle, and compost. Recycle City also promotes environmental justice. Justice means fair treatment and environmental justice means making sure we all have a fair chance of living the healthiest life possible, no matter what we look like or where we grew up.

Just click on any section of Recycle City that you want to tour. You can try the new Recycle City Scavenger Hunt, try the Activities or play the cool games!

When you leave, you'll know much more about what you can do to help protect people and the environment. Have fun!
